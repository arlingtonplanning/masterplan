Median Single Family Home Sales Price

The chart shown here shows the median sales price for single family home in Arlington from 1990 through 2015. Hover over bars for more information. Data has been adjusted for inflation using tables from the Bureau of Labor Statistics. All data is from The Warren Group
Most communities in the region witnessed a decline in housing sale prices during the most recent recession period, but in Arlington they actually increased by 3.1% between 2006 and 2012.
